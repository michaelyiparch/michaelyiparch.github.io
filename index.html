Year 3 Studio · 2026

The Aero-Matrix

High-Rise Porosity System · San Sau Street, Tuen Mun, Hong Kong

Structure

Core & Module Integration

The Aero-Matrix uses a permanent central core for stability and vertical circulation. MiC modules plug in around it — allowing strategic empty slots for cross-ventilation through the building.

Separating "permanent" from "adaptable" elements is what makes the hollow modules possible — the lungs of the building.

Atmosphere

Three Scales of Living

Modular Void — Urban Porosity
Communal Heart — The Social Matrix
Private Unit — Modular Living
Synthesis

The Aero-Matrix demonstrates that high-density housing need not be a barrier to the urban environment. Through strategic hollow modules, MiC efficiency, and community-centered design, the project offers a scalable, breathing architecture that benefits both occupants and the city.
